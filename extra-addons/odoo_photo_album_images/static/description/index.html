Company Photo Album Manage in Odoo

Photo / Image Albums Manage in Odoo.

This module allows your photo album team to create photo albums and upload photos/images on it as shown in below screenshots.


Main Features:
  • Allow your Album Team to create and manage photo albums.
  • You can see Photos in Albums in a separate menu.
  • Allow your Album Team to create and manage Album Tags.
  • Allow your Album Manager to create and manage Album Types.
  • Send by Email functionality on photo album form.
  • PDF report of photo album.
  • Basically this app allows you to record your photos album for different events and activities of your company.
  • For more details please check below screenshots and watch the video.

Menus Available:

  • Albums
    • Photo Albums
    • Photos in Album
    • Configuration
      • Album Tags
      • Album Types
