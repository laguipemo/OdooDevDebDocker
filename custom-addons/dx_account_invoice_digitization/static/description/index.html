OCR Invoice Digitalization

Functionality:

"Invoice Digitalization" streamlines manual work by automatically adding identifiable descriptions for text fields in Odoo. It uses OCR to extract information, updates various fields, and configures different AI models. The module enhances productivity and efficiency by generating automatic responses within the Odoo social network and other applications.

  • If the customer exists it is selected on the invoice
  • If the client does not exist, the module provides the option to create it
  • If the product exists, it is selected on the invoice lines
  • If the product does not exist, the module offers the option to create it
  • The module uses a search algorithm to locate both products and customers when the similarity exceeds 80% Example: Azure Interior => Asure Interior




Benefits:

  • Saves Time: Automates the process of digitizing invoices, significantly reducing manual effort and saving valuable time.
  • Cost-Efficient: Reduces the costs associated with manual data entry and minimizes the risk of errors, leading to overall cost savings.
  • Improved Accuracy: Utilizes OCR technology and AI to enhance accuracy in extracting and populating invoice details.
  • Streamlined Processes: Streamlines the workflow by automatically updating Odoo records with extracted information.
  • Configurable: Allows users to configure AI models and update various fields according to specific business requirements.
